# How to Find Your Railway URL

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Option 1: Click on the Service
1. **Click on the "web" service card** (the card on the right showing "Active")
2. This opens the service details page
3. Look for **"Domains"** or **"Generate Domain"** button
4. Click it to get your URL

### Option 2: Settings â†’ Domains
1. Click **"Settings"** button (in the "web" service card)
2. Look for **"Domains"** in the left sidebar
3. Click **"Generate Domain"**
4. Copy the URL it creates

### Option 3: Check the Top Bar
1. Look at the very top of the Railway page
2. There might be a URL displayed there
3. Or a "View" or "Open" button

### Option 4: Service Overview
1. Click on **"web"** service
2. In the service page, look for:
   - A URL displayed at the top
   - A "Public URL" section
   - A "Domains" tab

## What to Look For

The URL will look like:
```
https://soundflakes-production-xxxx.up.railway.app
```

Or:
```
https://web-production-xxxx.up.railway.app
```

## If You Still Can't Find It

Try this:
1. Click **"Settings"** (gear icon) in the "web" service card
2. Scroll down to find **"Domains"**
3. Click **"Generate Domain"**

---

**Note:** Sometimes Railway generates a domain automatically. Check the deployment logs or the service overview page.
